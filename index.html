Vikasa Tech GSoC Ideas List

The ideas for Vikasa Tech GSoC 2024.

1. Introduction:
At Vikasa Tech, we aim to solve real-world problems in domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Blockchain, and Gamifying technology. Vikasa Tech, the parent organization, oversees several child organizations each focusing on specific domains.

2. How to Use This List:
Consider this list as a starter guide for choosing projects and domains to contribute to. It offers a variety of projects across our organization and its subsidiaries.

3. Project Ideas:

Under Vikasa Tech, we have:

Title: Intern: Revolutionizing Internships

Description: We provide internships to students based on their understanding and level of expertise.

Expected Hours:350 Hours

Difficulty Rating:Hard

Expected Outcomes:
i. An internship website that assesses students through AI-based interviews and offers them projects or industrial-grade projects.
ii. An AI-based learning platform with training and roadmaps, and live classes if required.
iii. Upskilling students with industry-based live projects and learning.

Skills Required/Preferred: HTML/CSS and React.js, Databases like MongoDB, MySQL, Cloud services like Google Cloud and AWS, Generative AI, Chatbots, Machine Learning, Python, Java, Node.js for the backend/ Postman or Rest APIs, UI/UX or at least Figma, Adobe Illustrator (not mandatory but a plus point).

Difficulty Level:
i. Frontend Development: Medium
ii. Backend Development: Hard
iii. AI Interviews Feature Full Development (Frontend, Backend, AI Model): Hard
iv. Doubt clearance/ Training with our customized Courses and Roadmaps for the user: Hard

Under Chainz, a Vikasa Tech subsidiary:

Title: ChainzRegistry: Revolutionizing Land Transactions

Description: This project aims to modernize land registration, making the process transparent, tamper-proof, and secure. It utilizes tamper-resistant ledger technology and IPFS for document safety.

Expected Hours:150 Hours

Difficulty Rating:Medium

Key Features: Online transactions, AI/ML for price analysis, E-Vault, Reward system, Bored button.

Expected Outcomes:
i. Revolutionizing land transactions.
ii. Making Web 3 more mainstream.
iii. Enhancing the security of property buying and selling.

Skills Required/Preferred: Flutter and React.js, Databases like MongoDB and IPFS like Pinata and NFT.Storage, Cloud services like Google Cloud and AWS, Machine learning models which predict data, Java and Advanced Java, like Swings, FX, Node.js for the backend, Solidity, Good knowledge about DAO, Defi and Blockchain, Hardhat, Polygon/Matic, UI/UX or at least Figma, Adobe Illustrator (not mandatory but a plus point).

Difficulty Level:
i. Frontend Development: Medium
ii. Backend Development: Hard
iii. Predictive ML Model: Medium

Under Baat Cheet, we have:

Title:BaatCheet: For Businesses

Description:A platform where business people meet for potential collab or funding.

Expected Hours:90 Hours

Difficulty Rating:Medium

Expected Outcomes:
A platform where we aim to develop a platform designed to facilitate networking among business professionals and assist newcomers in securing funding. This service will feature the option of engaging in spontaneous video calls or scheduling them in advance. A key feature of our platform is the integration of advanced AI technology, which will be responsible for verifying the authenticity of participants and ensuring the alignment of interactions with users' preferences. For instance, if a user specifies a preference to connect with male participants, the AI will ensure that this preference is accurately met, thereby optimizing the relevance and efficiency of the networking experience.

Skills Required/Preferred: HTML/CSS and React.js, Databases like MongoDB, MySQL, Cloud services like Google Cloud and AWS, Generative AI, Machine Learning, Python, Java, Node.js for the backend/ Postman or Rest APIs, UI/UX or at least Figma, Adobe Illustrator (not mandatory but a plus point).

Difficulty Level:
i. Frontend Development: Medium
ii. Backend Development: Hard
iii. AI Model: Hard
